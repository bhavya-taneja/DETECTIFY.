FAQ

1What are dark patterns in e-commerce?

Deceptive user interface design practices intended to trick users into making unintended choices.

2 How do dark patterns impact online shoppers?

They manipulate user behavior, leading to unintended actions, purchases, or personal data disclosures.

3Examples of common dark patterns?

Sneak into basket, bait and switch, misdirection, hidden costs, and forced continuity.

4 Why do e-commerce sites use dark patterns?

To boost sales, capture user data, or influence decisions for the benefit of the business.

5 How can users identify dark patterns?

Watch for misleading prompts, unclear opt-ins, rushed decision-making, and hidden terms.

6Are dark patterns illegal?

Some are, as they violate consumer protection laws by being deceptive or misleading.

7 Can dark patterns be reported?

Yes, users can report them to relevant authorities or through consumer protection channels.
