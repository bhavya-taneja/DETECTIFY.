Detectify Guard Against Dark Patterns

In a digital landscape full of traps, having Dark Pattern Detection can make all the difference. Discover why savvy users turn to this invaluable tool to safeguard their online experience and unlock a secure digital journey.

Subscription Trap

A subscription trap deceives users with the promise of free services, then ensnares them in costly subscriptions. Hidden terms complicate escape, turning what seemed like a good deal into an unwanted financial commitment.

Bait and Switch

Bait and switch online tricks users by advertising one thing but delivering another. It's a crafty move that capitalizes on initial interest, leaving users with an unexpected and often less appealing outcome.

Drip Pricing

Drip pricing slyly reveals hidden fees during your online purchase journey. Starting with a seemingly lower cost, these additional charges 'drip' in, surprising users and undermining the transparency of the transaction.

False Urgency

False urgency is a sneaky website tactic, using fake time constraints to push users into hurried decisions. It preys on the fear of missing out, leading to impulsive choices that users might regret later.

Nagging

Nagging on websites is the persistent, digital equivalent of constant nudges. Through continuous prompts and reminders, it aims to wear down resistance, turning a smooth online experience into an irritating interaction.

Confirm Shaming

Confirm shaming is a crafty website trick, using guilt-inducing prompts to pressure users into specific actions. It leverages subtle psychological tactics to influence decisions through discomfort.
